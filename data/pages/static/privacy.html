Privacy Policy

Defitions

We define personally identifiable information as any information which can be related another peice of information — that is, any information which cannot provide any information to relate it to any other piece of information — regardless if this information cannot be related directly back to you as a person. This should be compatible with any reasonable defition of PII, including the European Union's very general definition in the GDPR.

Information we collect

We need to collect some information from you in order to operate our site. Much of this information is personally identifiable.

Here is a list of data we collect, where we collect it and why we do so:

  • Text, writings and paragraphs written by you. We collect this data in comments, mod pages, wiki pages and articles. We need to collect this data to display it to other users as this is the main content of intrest.
  • Password hashes and account info. We collect this data nearly everywhere on the site. We need to collect this data as the site cannot function without it.
  • Logs of actions, including dates, times and other metadata. We collect this data any time you ubmit a form, including things like comments, log-ins and account creations. We need to collect this data in order to show when certian actions have been preformed in order to provide context to the reader and to staff.
  • Internal software identifiers. We collect this data upon logging in, using comments, registering accounts and in other places. We need to collect this data so the site software knows how to do things like find comments and check which user you are signed in to.
  • Various other data points. We collect this data upon logging in, using comments, registering accounts and in other places. We need to collect this data so the site software knows how to do things like find comments and check which user you are signed in to.

In general, if you see information on the site, we have to store that somehow in order to display it back to you. This might involve the use of extra, random data that can also be assocaited with you.

We do not knowingly collect or process information of anyone under the age of 16 as we do not feel we can properly secure their data as required by the GDPR. Any use of an account by a person under the age of 16 may result in account and information deletion.

Note: If we suspect the email address used to register belongs to the child's parents or current caretaker, a report may be sent to that email advising of the incident.

Data we process

We process both stored and temporary data in numerous ways, and we are constantly adding new ways to process data.

We may not be able to list everything; however, here are many of the ways we process data:

  • All data is subject to sanitisation and validation, which are ways we keep malicous actors from harming the site's security.
  • For texts, we may do things like converting the rich text syntax to display formatted text.
  • We may transform data to make it more secure to store, or we may simply process data to display it onscreen.
  • We may format data in new ways, or convert between different formats. For example, we might change the format of a date from 24/6/2014 to 2014-06-24.

Cookies

This site uses Cookies and the Web Storage API to store account details about logged in users which are required to use the site. We do not use cookies for tracking or any other reason at the moment.

We do not store cookies if you are not logged in. If you do not want any cookies, please do not log in or create an account.
